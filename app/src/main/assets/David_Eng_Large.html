David Terzyan

Orchestra Director, Deputy Director

D. Terzyan was a graduate of Yerevan State Pedagogical Institute. He has been the Orchestra Director of Serenade Chamber Orchestra from 1991. Since 2000 he is the Head of Concerts and Tours Department of the Armenian National Philharmonic Orchestra. Since 2003 he is the Deputy Director of Aram Khachaturian Concert Hall and Armenian National Philharmonic Orchestra. He organized numerous tours of Armenian soloists, music groups, orchestras and vocal ensembles. Among them to Cyprus, Czech Republic, Germany, Slovakia, Japan, Italy, UAE, Lebanon, France, Greece, Hungary, Poland, Austria, Russia and Switzerland. All together more than 800 concerts in Europe were organized through his participation. He is credited with annual organization of Yerevan International Music Festival since 2007, Yerevan Viennese Music Festival in 2000, and Japan Music Festival in Yerevan in 2001.
