Ruzanna Sirunyan

Managing Director

R. Sirunyan was a graduate of Yerevan State Institute of National Economy, Faculty of Economics. Since 2005 she is a Managing Director of the Armenian National Philharmonic Orchestra. Prior to this position she served as Deputy Director there for 5 years and had a considerable experience working as Chief Accountant with Yerevan Komitas Chamber Music Hall, Cadence Music Center and Armenian State Philharmonia. R. Sirunyan has been the financial manager of numerous projects and festivals: Yerevan International Music Festival, Arts and Culture Management Course, Stephan Elmas Competition, World Music Festival, Chilingirian Violin, Viola and Cello Competition, All Armenian Piano Competition, Week of Kurt Weill in Armenia, Human Rights Day in Armenia and others. In 2012 she was awarded the Golden Medal of the Ministry of Culture of Armenia and since 2013 she is the Merited Worker of Culture of Armenia. She is a deputy of Yerevan Magistrate since 2013.
